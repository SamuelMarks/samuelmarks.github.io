RoiAlign
========

Region of Interest (RoI) align operation described in the [Mask R-CNN paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.06870). RoiAlign consumes an input tensor X and region of interests (rois) to apply pooling across each RoI; it produces a 4-D tensor of shape (num_rois, C, output_height, output_width). RoiAlign ...
