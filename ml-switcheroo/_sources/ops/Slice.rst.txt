Slice
=====

Produces a slice of the input tensor along multiple axes. Similar to numpy: https://numpy.org/doc/stable/user/basics.indexing.html?highlight=slice#slicing-and-striding Slice uses the `starts`, `ends`, `axes` and `steps` inputs to select a sub-tensor of its input `data` tensor.
